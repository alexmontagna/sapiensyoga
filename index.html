what is sapiens yoga?

sapiens yoga is an updated version of old traditions adapted to modern life in a sanskrit-less fashion

why sapiens yoga?

this is a project made to inspire who seeks self development in a modern way.

after thousands of years, we know better.

what is the goal?

it's time to have a label that offers the anthology of yoga, embracing all good new sapiens knowledge of the last centuries.

what is the method?

with predominantly free body exercises and basic breathing techniques for fitness, the method aims to improve emotions and thoughts through pleasant mindfulness and relaxation moments.

how can i taste it?

at the moment you can try it through private classes in milan, retreats in italy, or online groups.
